Plot
Keeper of the lost cites(kotlc) follows a 12 year old girl named Sophie. She gets whisked away to a magical elf land where she finds she was hidden in the human world to be used as a weapon to save the elves. Though she is young Sophie has to make many difficult decisions and learn quickly how to adjust. She makes lots of friends a long the way, including the ones that have passed in her jorney. The story is incredibly captivating with many plot twists.

Sophie Foster

Sophie Foster. The main character of our story. She starts out as a 12 year old girl hiding amongst humans, growing up believing she was one of them. Though she had a secret, she was a telepath. Hiding this from all her loved ones and getting it revealed to her that she is an Elf. She gets uprooted from her life moving to a new world. Her old family doesn't remember her, she is in the elven world needing to throw away everything that she's ever known. Along the story she faces many challenges. She has to deal with immense pressures, trying to figure out who made her, who keeps trying to kill her. She has to deal with being an outsider and being hated by so many people. In the latest book she is about 15-16, and she has lost so many friends. Murder, sacrifcing, and the only support system she's ever had falling apart. She learns in about book 3 that she was created by a group called the black swan to be the ultimate weapon against the neverseen, this causes her to spiral more than once in the series. She gets closer to them even running away to them at one point. She gets critized often for making rash decisions, but she is still a child. Who was forced into this role and has unbelievable amounts of pressure on her shoulders.
