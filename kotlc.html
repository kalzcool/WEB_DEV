Keeper of the lost cities

Plot
Keeper of the lost cites(kotlc) follows a 12 year old girl named Sophie. She gets whisked away to a magical elf land where she finds she was hidden in the human world to be used as a weapon to save the elves. Though she is young Sophie has to make many difficult decisions and learn quickly how to adjust. She makes lots of friends a long the way, including the ones that have passed in her jorney. The story is incredibly captivating with many plot twists.
