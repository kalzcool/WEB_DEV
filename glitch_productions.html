Glitch productions

Glitch productions is an Indie animation studio founded in 2017 by brothers Kevin and Luke Lerdwichagul in Sydney, Australia from their bedroom. They started it with one goal in mind, "Change the way that the west creates teen and young adult animation". Since then the company has grown massivley. With only a few shows they have garnered millions of fans gloabally. Despite everything they remain fully self-funded and indipendant from major compamies. The companies shows so far include:

  1. Murder drones
  2. Meta Runner
  3. Sunset Paridice
  4. The Amazing digital circus
  5. Gaslight district
  6. lackadaisy
  7. Knights of Guinevere
